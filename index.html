How To Make cereal


Ingredients Method

Ingredients

  • medium sized bowl
  • required amount of cereal
  • spoon
  • 125mL of milk

Method

  1. put the bowl on the scales
  2. turn the scales on
  3. pour in reqiured amount of cereal
  4. pour 125ml of milk into the bowl with cereal
  5. place the spoon in the bowl

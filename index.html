How To Make cereal


Ingredients Method

Ingredients

  • medium sized bowl
  • cereal and how much cereal is needed on the side of the box
  • spoon
  • 125mL of milk

Method

  1. put the bowl on the scales
  2. turn the scales on
  3. pour the cereal into the bowl to the set amount of grams it's says on the box
  4. pour 125ml of milk into the bowl with cereal
  5. place the spoon in the bowl
